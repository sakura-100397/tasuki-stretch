# W600×H910mm 屋外看板作成プロンプト

## 目的

3つ折りパンフレット2種の内容を最大限に盛り込み、屋外店頭に置く縦型看板画像を作成するためのプロンプト。

参照元:

- `3つ折りパンフ1修正会員限定.pdf`
- `3つ折りパンフ2修正IBJが提供する.pdf`

## 画像生成用プロンプト

```text
Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: outdoor standing sign / storefront A-frame sign
Primary request:
Create a single-sided vertical outdoor sign image for TASUKI STRETCH, based on the referenced Japanese tri-fold brochure. The sign must fit W600mm × H910mm and include as much of the brochure content as possible while remaining readable from outdoors.

Canvas:
Portrait sign, W600mm × H910mm, aspect ratio 60:91.
Recommended output size: 3543 × 5374 px at 150dpi or higher.
Include 3mm bleed and keep all important text inside a 25mm safe margin.
No fold lines. This is one continuous vertical sign.

Overall design:
Elegant Japanese beauty and matchmaking service sign. Use warm ivory Japanese paper texture, thin antique-gold outer border, subtle gold corner ornament, and soft flowing gold wave ribbons near the bottom. Premium, calm, clean, bridal/matchmaking beauty atmosphere. Use cream, antique gold, rose pink, sage green, and sumi black.

Color palette:
Sumi black: #2f2924
Antique gold: #a77735
Rose pink: #cf5f79
Sage green: #4f8276
Soft ivory: #fbf7ef
Light rose background: #f9e7eb
Light sage background: #e8f2ec

Typography:
Use Japanese Mincho-style type for headlines, bold and elegant.
Use a readable Japanese serif or Gothic type for smaller body copy.
Outdoor readability is important: large headline, clear section labels, no tiny decorative text except legal-like notes if needed.
All Japanese text must be accurate and legible.

Layout:
Create one vertical composition with 7 stacked zones.

ZONE 1: Top brand and badges

Composition notes:
Prioritize hierarchy:
1. Main headline
2. Two services
3. Special prices
4. QR reservation
5. Access

Outdoor sign readability:
Avoid tiny body text. Use short line lengths. Use high contrast between text and background. Keep QR codes large enough to scan from close range. Use rounded cards and color-coded sections to organize dense information.

Constraints:
Do not change the Japanese text.
Do not invent new QR codes.
Do not add fake logos, fake addresses, fake phone numbers, or extra disclaimers.
Do not make it look like a gym, Western fitness flyer, medical clinic ad, or app UI.
Do not use dark backgrounds, neon colors, heavy shadows, cluttered layout, or low-contrast text.
The final image must look like a premium Japanese matchmaking beauty conditioning sign derived from the brochure.

Avoid:
Incorrect Japanese, garbled characters, unreadable QR codes, missing prices, wrong address, excessive text cramming, cartoon style, stock-poster look, cheap discount flyer style.
```
